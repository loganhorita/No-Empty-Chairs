Logan M.M. Horita is the Founder and Editor-in-Chief of No Empty Chairs, a blog dedicated to fostering faith-centered reflections and encouraging intentional choices in media and life. Rooted in his deep love for the gospel and family, Logan created No Empty Chairs to help others navigate their spiritual journey while keeping their focus on what matters most: their faith, relationships, and eternal perspective.
